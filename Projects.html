Projects 2020

  • Trash Classifier
    • As a part of the CS344/CS386 Course Projecte I worked with my team on a trash detection pipeling using the TACO dataset. Github
  • MIT India Initiative: Medical Non Compliance System Design
    • I was selected to attend the MIT India Initiative 2020 for the theme "Community-based health solution", where I worked with a team of fellow students to brainstorm and develop a Medical Non Compliance System during the workshop. The prototype aimed to overcome the problem faced by families with elderly patients that need to be monitored for their medication intake.
  • CS 218 Course Project: Application of Network Flow Algorithms in Transport System
    • I worked on a team of 2 for developing an application of network flow algorithms in the Bus Transportation system for Shahapur Taluka. We used data obtain from ticket purchases and records to map travellers at various bus stations. The intake of travellers at various time and location pairs were used to model a flow and the bus networks were mapped to a graph. The network flow algorithm was applied to obtain a occupancy analysis of the current bus infrastructure.
